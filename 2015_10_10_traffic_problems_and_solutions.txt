# Vocabulary
Congested (adj) /kənˈdʒes.tɪd/: too crowed, trafix jam, too ​blocked or ​crowded
Commuter (n) /kəˈmjuː.tər/: someone travel from work to home by public tranportation
Rush hour (n) /rʌʃ aʊər/: the moment's crowed
Frustrating (adj) /frʌsˈtreɪ.tɪŋ/: make someone annoyed
Integrate (v) /ˈɪn.tɪ.ɡreɪt/: add some thing to another thing
Metropolitan (adj) /ˌmet.rəˈpɒl.ɪ.tən/: people in city

# Idoms
To be stuck in traffic (idiom): to be caught in a traffic jam
To tie traffic up (idiom): to cause road traffic to stop

# Structure, Ways of expressing solutions:
